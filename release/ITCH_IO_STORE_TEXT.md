# ArenaForge

ArenaForge is a browser-playable auto-battler inspired by Teamfight Tactics.

Build your board, combine units into stronger starred versions, manage gold, and watch your team fight automatically.

## Current features

- Shop, reroll, XP, and lock flow
- Bench and board positioning
- Traits and synergies
- Item inventory, crafting, and equip flow
- Auto-combat with visible unit feedback
- Star-upgrades from duplicate units
- Draft, armory, encounter, elite, and boss rounds
- NPC opponents with different styles and formations

## Current scope

This is a playable prototype release. Core systems are in place, while balance, presentation, and content breadth are still being improved.

## Controls

- Mouse: buy, place, select, equip
- Best played in landscape
- Mouse is fully supported
- Touch support is partially prepared

## Notes

- Best played in landscape
- Browser release target: itch.io HTML build
- Designed for `1280x720` and above
