# ArenaForge

ArenaForge is a prototype auto-battler inspired by Teamfight Tactics.

Build your board, combine units into stronger starred versions, manage gold, and watch your team fight automatically.

## Current features

- Shop, reroll, XP, and lock flow
- Bench and board positioning
- Traits and synergies
- Item inventory, crafting, and equip flow
- Auto-combat with visible unit feedback
- Star-upgrades from duplicate units

## Current scope

This is an early playable build. Systems are in place, but balance, presentation, and content breadth are still in active development.

## Controls

- Mouse: buy, place, select, equip
- Touch support is partially prepared, but desktop is the recommended first platform

## Notes

- Best played in landscape
- First public release target: Windows build
