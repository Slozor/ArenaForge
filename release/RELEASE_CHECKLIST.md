- Verify one full run from main menu to end screen
- Verify shop, bench, board, and item interactions
- Verify restart flow after defeat or victory
- Verify browser build loads without missing assets
- Verify `1280x720` layout

- Verify How To Play text is readable
- Verify Credits and Licenses are available in Settings
- Verify upload archive is `build/ArenaForge-web.zip`
